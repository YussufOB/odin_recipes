Egusi soup

Egusi is all tribe soup in Nigeria. It is know for it nice smell and lots of vitamins food items usually contains.

Ingredient(s):

  • Seasonings
  • Palm oil
  • Pepper
  • Fish and assorted meat
  • Locust bean (Iru)
  • Vegetable leaves
  • Powder melon

Steps:

  1. Fry pepper with the assorted meat/fish
  2. Add the melon powder and allow to cook for about 20mins
  3. Add the vegetable leaves then allow to steam for 5mins (min)
