Abula soup

Abula is a Yoruba tribe soup in Nigeria. It is know for it nice smell and lots of vitamins food items usually contains.

Ingredient(s):

  • Seasonings
  • Palm oil
  • Pepper
  • Fish and assorted meat
  • Locust bean (Iru)
  • cooked meshed beans
  • Ewedu leaves

Steps:

  1. Make stew with the assorted meat/fish and the pepper (seperately)
  2. Make Ewedu soup with the leaves and the Locust beans
  3. Make Gbegiri soup with the cooked meshed beans and palm oil.
